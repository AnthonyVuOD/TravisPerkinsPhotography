hi. howdy. hello.

My name is Travis Perkins and I am a photographer. At a young age I discovered the joy of serving others and a passion for photographs. My goal is to reveal authentic culture, tradition, and humanity through the digital art form. I believe that every new day is a gift, and I want nothing more than to feel and experience the wild earth with my own two hands. I have experience in weddings, special events, travel, music venues, landscape, animals, and expecially the outdoors. I have been featured in Do512.com, Austin Fit Magazine, Austin Monthly, amongst other publications. I believe in the strength of community across the spectrum and that the best of life is experienced when shared with others. I'd consider it a privilege to cover your event and capture the memories through the art of photography.
